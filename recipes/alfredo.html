Alfredo Sauce

This Alfredo sauce recipe always turns out rich and creamy! A delicious way to transform plain fettuccine pasta or steamed vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmasan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic crushed
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh Parsley

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add cheese and garlic; whisk continuously until heated through. Stir in parsley and serve.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
